Bundled fonts and their licenses
=================================

Anton-Regular.ttf
  Anton by Vernon Adams / Igino Marini.
  Licensed under the SIL Open Font License v1.1 (see OFL.txt).
  Used for burned-in captions.

NotoColorEmoji.ttf
  Noto Color Emoji by Google.
  Copyright 2022 Google Inc. Licensed under the SIL Open Font License v1.1.
  Source: https://github.com/googlefonts/noto-emoji
  Used by the auto-emoji caption feature (B4) to rasterize emoji glyphs,
  since the caption font (Anton) contains no emoji glyphs.

Both fonts ship in-repo so a fresh clone renders fully offline on Windows
and Ubuntu (no system fonts required), per the project's local-first rule.
